ABOUT THE EVENT

36 Hours of Hacking

Bring your ideas to life under tight timelines. Buildathon '26 is an intense 36-hour college-wide hackathon bringing developers, designers, and managers together to build solutions for real-world problems.

Teams of 2

Collaborate closely. Register either as an Individual or as a Team of up to 2 members. Share codes, leverage strengths, and deliver a clean working application prototype.

Secure Entry Passes

Submit your profiles, portfolios, and GitHub links. Once approved by the administrator panel, download your entry passes containing unique attendance scanning QR codes.

EVENT TIMELINE

Phase 1

Registrations Open

Sign in using your authorized college email address, update your profiles with GitHub/LinkedIn URLs, and create/join teams.

Phase 2

Admin Profile Verification

Admin panel reviews social profiles, portfolios, and register numbers. Selected applicants receive confirmation entry passes.

Phase 3

QR Pass Download

Approved participants download their entry passes containing encrypted attendance check-in tokens.

Phase 4

Hack Day & Attendance Check-in

Scan your QR Pass at the venue entrance. Admin registers check-ins cleanly. Duplicate scans are blocked automatically.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who can participate in Buildathon?

+

Participation is restricted to students registered in our college who possess a valid college email address ending with the configured domain. Off-campus domain email addresses are rejected automatically.

What is the maximum team capacity?

+

Teams can have a maximum of 2 members. You can also participate as an individual. Individual registrations do not require joining a team.

How does the check-in scanner work?

+

Once approved, your entry pass will show a QR Code containing a unique UUID. The venue admin will scan this code on hack day to log your attendance. Duplicate scans will trigger warnings and prevent double registration.
